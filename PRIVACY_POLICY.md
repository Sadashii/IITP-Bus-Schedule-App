# Privacy Policy for IITP Bus Schedule

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the **IITP Bus Schedule** application (the "App"), created by Tanishq ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") as a hobbyist project for college students. This policy explains what information our App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any sensitive, personal, or identifiable user data.** 

The App operates almost entirely locally on your device. It does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information. 

## 3. Required Permissions Explained
The App requests the following permissions strictly to provide core functionality:
- **Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`)**: Required exclusively to download the latest bus schedule from a publicly available Google Sheet/CSV file. No data about your usage, device, or identity is sent back to any servers.
- **Boot Completed (`android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`)**: Required strictly to ensure that widget alarm updates and schedules automatically resume working correctly after you restart your phone.
- **Exact Alarms (`android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`)**: Required to ensure time tracking inside the app is completely accurate, especially for widget updates.
- **Vibration (`android.permission.VIBRATE`)**: Used solely for providing haptic feedback (small vibrations) when interacting with buttons, chips, and cards inside the application.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App fetches scheduling data from a Google Spreadsheet interface. Google may collect standard networking logs (like IP addresses) when your device requests this public document, which is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Children's Privacy
Because we do not collect any personal data, our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any modifications are effective immediately upon being posted on this page.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer directly or open an "issue" on the project's public repository.
